All India Consumer Price Index of Milk & Products, Oils & Fats, Fruits and Vegetables from January 2023 to May 2023

Consumer Price Index (CPI) measures changes over time in the general level of prices of goods and services that households acquire for the purpose of consumption. For the construction of CPI numbers, two requisite components are weighing diagrams (consumption patterns) and price data collected at regular intervals. The data refers to the All India Consumer Price Index with the base year 2012 equal to 100 and combined for both rural and urban areas.

  • All India Consumer Price Index (CPI) - Milk and products increased from 174.6 in January 2023 to 179.5 in May 2023, showing an increase of 4.9 points.
  • All India Consumer Price Index (CPI) - Oils and fats decreased from 187.2 in January 2023 to 170 in May 2023, showing a decrease of 17.2 points.
  • All India Consumer Price Index (CPI) - Fruits increased from 158.3 in January 2023 to 172.2 in May 2023, showing an increase of 13.9 points.
  • All India Consumer Price Index (CPI) - Vegetables increased from 152.7 in February 2023 to 161 in May 2023, showing an increase of 8.3 points.
